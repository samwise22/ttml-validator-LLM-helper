# Curated Source Register

Only records with status `verified` may be cited by generated reports. Verification means that a maintainer opened the exact URL, confirmed the title and relevant content, and recorded the date. A reachable parent page does not verify a guessed fragment or deep link.

## Adding a source

A source record **shall** include a stable ID, exact page title, exact deep URL, verification date, a concise maintainer-authored paraphrase, and—only when needed—a short verified quotation. The reviewer **shall** check that the link reaches the relevant section without relying on search-result snippets.

BBC content is supporting editorial guidance. It does not supersede the validator and shall not be represented as validator output.
