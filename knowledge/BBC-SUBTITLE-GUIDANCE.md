# BBC Subtitle Guidance Knowledge Base

This file is intentionally conservative in the foundation release. The BBC subtitle guide root is registered in `SOURCES.md`, but automated access was blocked during initial research and no deep link has yet passed the repository's manual verification rule.

Until entries are verified:

- generated reports shall not quote the BBC guide;
- generated reports shall not cite guessed fragments or deep URLs; and
- analysis may explain validator findings without attaching an editorial citation.

Future curated entries should be organized by topic (timing, line breaks, speaker identification, sound effects, music, punctuation, positioning, and readability) and should contain concise paraphrases rather than a wholesale copy of the source guide.
